The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time is the fifth Legend of Zelda game made. It was released in the US on November 23, 1998. It was originaly for the Nintendo 64 (N64), but has been rereleased twice. It was released a second time for the GameCube on February 18, 2003. A third time it was released for the 3DS.
